CLINICAL STYLE GUIDE (DEMO / EDUCATIONAL)

This style guide is for AI-assisted drafts only. AI output is not final.
Clinician review and approval are required before use.

1) Patient-Friendly Language Rules
1. Use plain language and avoid jargon.
2. Use short sentences and one idea per sentence.
3. Prefer active voice and clear verbs.
4. Define any necessary medical terms in simple words.

2) Instructions Format
1. Use numbered steps for patient instructions.
2. Keep each step brief and actionable.
3. Include who to contact for questions.

3) Mandatory Follow-Up and Red Flags
1. Always include follow-up timing or appointment details.
2. Always list red flags that require urgent care.

4) AI Assistive-Only Disclaimer
- AI drafts are assistive and not final clinical documentation.
- Clinician review is required before saving or sharing.
