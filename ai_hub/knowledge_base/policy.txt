HOSPITAL SOP AND POLICY (DEMO / EDUCATIONAL)

This policy document is for demo and educational use only.
All AI outputs are assistive drafts and require clinician review and sign-off.

1) DISCHARGE SUMMARY SOP (REQUIRED SECTIONS)
1.1 Required Sections Checklist (discharge summary policy)
1. Diagnosis
2. Treatment summary
3. Medication list
4. Follow-up plan
5. Red flags / return precautions
6. Sign-off

1.2 Diagnosis
- State the primary diagnosis and key secondary diagnoses.
- Include relevant problem list items.

1.3 Treatment Summary
- Summarize procedures, hospital course, and response to treatment.
- Note complications or significant events.

1.4 Medication List
- Provide a final medication list with dose, route, and frequency.
- Clearly label NEW, CHANGED, and STOPPED medications.

1.5 Follow-Up Plan
- Document follow-up appointments, timeframe, and specialty.
- Include required labs or imaging with timelines.

1.6 Red Flags / Return Precautions
- List red flags that require urgent evaluation.
- Include clear instructions for when to return to the ED.

1.7 Sign-Off
- Discharging clinician name, role, and signature.
- Date/time of discharge summary completion.

Policy keywords: discharge summary, SOP, required sections, medication list, follow-up, red flags, sign-off.

2) APPOINTMENT POLICY (NO-SHOWS, REMINDERS, RESCHEDULING)
2.1 No-Shows
- First no-show: send reminder and offer rescheduling.
- Repeated no-shows require admin review and documentation.

2.2 Reminders
- Send reminders 24-48 hours before the appointment.
- Include date, time, location, and preparation instructions.

2.3 Rescheduling
- Patients may reschedule up to 24 hours in advance.
- Same-day rescheduling is subject to availability.

Policy keywords: appointment policy, no-show, reminders, rescheduling.

3) BILLING / INVOICE POLICY
3.1 Required Invoice Fields
- Patient name, patient ID (if available).
- Date of service and invoice date.
- Itemized charges (room, doctor fee, medication, other).
- Total amount, payment status, and billing contact.

3.2 Disputes
- Disputes must be logged within 30 days.
- Provide a written explanation and supporting documents.

3.3 Refunds
- Refunds require admin approval and documentation.
- Record refund reason and amount in billing notes.

Policy keywords: billing policy, invoice, disputes, refunds.

4) DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY
4.1 Minimum Necessary Data
- Include only the minimum necessary data in AI drafts and notes.
- Avoid unnecessary PII when patient ID is sufficient.

4.2 Access Control
- Only authorized staff can access discharge summaries.
- Audit access and edits when possible.

4.3 Clinician Review Required
- AI outputs are not final clinical documents.
- Clinician review and sign-off are mandatory before saving or sharing.

Policy keywords: privacy, security, clinician review, AI disclaimer, minimum necessary.
